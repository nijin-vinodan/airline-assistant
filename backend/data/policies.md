# Airline Policies

## Baggage Allowance
- **Carry-on**: 1 bag up to 7kg is free.
- **Checked Baggage**: Economy class allows 1 piece up to 23kg. Business class allows 2 pieces up to 32kg each.
- **Excess Baggage**: Charged at $50 per kg.

## Cancellation & Refund
- **24-hour rule**: Full refund if cancelled within 24 hours of booking, provided the flight is at least 7 days away.
- **Economy Saver**: Non-refundable.
- **Economy Flex**: Refundable with a $100 fee.
- **Business Class**: Fully refundable anytime before departure.

## Check-in
- **Online Check-in**: Opens 24 hours before departure and closes 1 hour before.
- **Airport Counter**: Opens 3 hours before and closes 60 minutes before departure.

## Pets
- Small pets (cats/dogs) are allowed in the cabin if the carrier fits under the seat (max 8kg with carrier).
- Large pets must travel in the cargo hold.
